Bakaula

Among the many places in Ranthambore National Park where you can spot the Royal Bengal Tiger & other wildlife during your Jeep Safari, one is Bakula. It is a densely forested region scattered with a number of small pools & water holes. During the summers, visitors can spot tigresses with their cubs visiting the water holes, along with other wild animals. The thick vegetation causes the air to retain moisture, thus making Bakula a relatively cool area compared to the other places in Ranthambore National Park. Carry a pair of binoculars and spot various wild animals in this area of the park.
